Created by Guillaume Arseneault, Joanne Comte, Charlène Gilbert et Hasmig Makdesian.



"Burst your bubble" is an interactive video projection that allows you and your friends to play with virtual bubble wrap.
 
Each projected bubble contains an animated sequence and a sound that are touch-enabled/activated by touch.
The experience reminds "sound boards" mechanisms and becomes a collective instrument enabled by the deformation of the projection surface.
 
Colorful , fun , collaborative and candid, « Burst your bubble » allows the viewer to let go and move up to an exciting lightness.
 
This installation is designed and approved by children from 3 to 99 years.
